NFL Positions Explained NFL位置详解

A comprehensive guide to all positions in American football 美式橄榄球所有位置的完整指南

Offense 进攻组 Defense 防守组 Special Teams 特勤组

NFL teams are divided into three main position groups: Offense, Defense, and Special Teams. Each group has specialized roles that work together to win games. NFL球队的位置被分为三个主要组别：进攻组（Offense）、防守组（Defense） 和 特勤组（Special Teams）。每个组别都有专门的角色，协同作战以赢得比赛。

Offense 进攻组

QB

Quarterback 四分卫

The quarterback is considered the leader of the offense. After receiving the ball from the center, the QB can choose to hand off to a running back, throw to a receiver, or run with the ball themselves. 四分卫通常被认为是进攻组的领袖。从中锋接球后，四分卫可以选择将球交给跑卫、传给外接手或自己跑球。

RB

Running Back 跑卫

Positioned in the backfield, the running back's primary task is to carry the ball and gain yards on the ground. They can also catch passes out of the backfield. 跑卫位于后场，主要任务是持球在地面推进码数。他们也可以从后场接球。

FB

Fullback 全卫

The fullback serves primarily as an additional blocker for the running back, clearing paths through the defense. They occasionally carry the ball in short-yardage situations. 全卫主要作为跑卫的额外阻挡者，为跑卫开辟通道。他们偶尔会在短码数情况下持球。

WR

Wide Receiver 外接手

Wide receivers catch forward passes from the quarterback. They can line up outside near the sideline or as a slot receiver closer to the center of the formation. 外接手接收四分卫的前传球。他们可以排在边线外侧，或作为槽接手（Slot Receiver）靠近中央阵型。

OL

Offensive Line 进攻锋线

The offensive line consists of the Center (C), two Guards (G), and two Tackles (T). They are responsible for protecting the quarterback and creating running lanes for the running back. 进攻锋线由中锋（Center）、两名护锋（Guards）和两名截锋（Tackles）组成，负责保护四分卫并为跑卫创造跑动通道。

C Center - Snaps the ball to QB 中锋 - 将球传给四分卫
G Guard - Protects interior gaps 护锋 - 保护内侧缺口
T Tackle - Protects outside edges 截锋 - 保护外侧边缘
TE

Tight End 近端锋

The tight end is a hybrid position that can block like a lineman and catch passes like a receiver. They line up next to the offensive tackle. 近端锋是混合型位置，既能像锋线球员那样阻挡，也能像接球手那样接球。他们排列在截锋旁边。

Defense 防守组

DT

Defensive Tackle 防守截锋

Defensive tackles line up on the interior of the defensive line. Their goal is to apply pressure up the middle, stop the run, and collapse the pocket on passing plays. 防守截锋排列在防守锋线内侧。他们的目标是在启球线后擒杀四分卫或阻止跑卫，负责施加内线压力。

DE

Defensive End 防守端锋

Defensive ends line up on the outside of the defensive line. They rush the quarterback from the edge and contain running plays from getting outside. 防守端锋排列在防守锋线外侧，从外侧冲击四分卫，并防止跑卫从外侧突破。

LB

Linebacker 线卫

Linebackers position behind the defensive line, usually three players. They are versatile defenders who must tackle running backs, cover tight ends, and occasionally rush the quarterback on blitz plays. 线卫位于防守锋线后方，通常有三名。他们需要多面手能力——擒抱跑卫、盯防近端锋，偶尔在突袭战术（Blitz）中冲击四分卫。

MLB Middle Linebacker - Defensive captain 中线卫 - 防守队长
OLB Outside Linebacker - Edge coverage 外线卫 - 边缘覆盖
CB

Cornerback 角卫

Cornerbacks line up on the outside edges of the defense. They are responsible for covering wide receivers and preventing them from catching passes. 角卫排在最外侧，负责防守外接手以阻止他们接球。

S

Safety 安全卫

Safeties primarily defend the deep areas of the field and serve as the last line of defense against the run. There are two types of safeties with distinct roles. 安全卫主要防守深区，也是防跑的最后防线。安全卫分为两种类型，各有不同职责。

SS Strong Safety - Closer to the line, run support 强卫 - 靠近启球线，支援防跑
FS Free Safety - Deepest defender, pass coverage 游卫 - 负责最深区域，传球防守

Special Teams 特勤组

K

Kicker 踢球员

The kicker is responsible for extra points after touchdowns (1 point) and field goals (3 points). They also perform kickoffs at the start of halves and after scores. 踢球员负责达阵后加分（1分）和射门得分（3分）。他们也负责半场开始和得分后的开球。

P

Punter 弃踢手

When the offense fails to convert on third down, the punter kicks the ball as far as possible to push the opposing team back and minimize their field position advantage. 在进攻失败后，弃踢手将球尽可能踢远，以压缩对手领地，减少对方的场地优势。

LS

Long Snapper 长传手

The long snapper specializes in snapping the ball back to the holder or punter on kicking plays. Accuracy and consistency are crucial for this specialized role. 长传手专门在踢球前将球传回给持球员或弃踢手。这个专业角色需要极高的准确性和稳定性。

H

Holder 持球员

The holder catches the snap and places the ball on the ground for the kicker on field goal and extra point attempts. This position is usually filled by the punter or a backup quarterback. 持球员为踢球员固定球，接住长传后将球放在地上。这个位置通常由弃踢手或替补四分卫担任。

KR/PR

Return Specialist 回攻手

Return specialists catch kickoffs (KR) or punts (PR) and attempt to advance the ball as far as possible. This role is usually filled by backup wide receivers, running backs, or cornerbacks who have exceptional speed. 回攻手接住对方踢出的球（开球回攻KR或弃踢回攻PR）并尽量向前推进。这个角色通常由速度出众的替补外接手、跑卫或角卫兼任。
